About Nexoz Studio

A studio built on craft, not trends.

Nexoz Studio opened with a simple belief: hair is architecture. Every consultation starts with your face shape, your lifestyle, and the texture you were born with — not a photo pulled from Pinterest.

Our stylists train continuously in color theory, cutting geometry, and finishing technique, so a visit to Doral's 7901 NW 36th St location feels less like a salon appointment and more like a private sitting with an artist who happens to specialize in you.
